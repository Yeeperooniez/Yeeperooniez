Probably the most important software on your computer will be your operatin system (OS). This is what everything runs on and it allows you to use your computer. When buying an operating system try and think about what you want to use the PC for. If you want to be very techy and do alot of coding then you might want to think about getting Linux. If you are only gaming then you should probably get windows because it is the most compatible with the most games. If you want to do prdutive wrok then you might want to get Mac OS (you can only get this if you already have a Mac). If you want to use two OSs or many OSs and you want to switch around then you should get a virtual machine. This will allow you to change your OS reguarly. Other imortant software might be things that you want to edit photo or video with. If you want some free professional software for video editing then I would reccomend DaVinchi Resolve and for photo editing you should use Photoshop, however there are many others that you can choose from (because you have to pay for photoshop) but these programs are very CPU heavy. Most editing software is very reliant on the CPU. If you are looking to stream your games on whatever platform then you will need some broadcasting software and like the editing software they are very CPU heavy. For streaming I reccomend either OBS Studio or Streamlabs OBS the OBS stands for "open broadcasting software". These programs will allow you to setup scenes and transitions and have game capture on your stream. If you want to render and make characters for games if you want to be a game develepor then Blender on Steam is great but in some cases might push you hardware to the limits. Sofware that contols your RGB lighting in your PC (if you have any) if very useful to have you can usually find the RGB software that you need by typing in the product you have and searcing for software for that product and finding its drivers. Antivirus is also a very imprtant peice of software and so is a VPN they both help you stay safe online. VPN stands for "virtual private network" and its stops people from getting your information online and they also allow you to get region restricted content. If you want to watch a Netflix show that is only availible in a certain country that you dont live in then you can you a VPN to get that restricted content. It also stops your ISP (internet service provider) from seeing your information and spying on you.

These are important peices of software but the most important are probably your OS, antivirus and VPN. Most of this software is availible for cheap on G2A. However always remember to check the reviews of the seller you are buying from on G2A
